# Hamster Ingame Combat Concept A

## Intent

`햄스터 키우기` 인게임 GameScene 시안. 로비/홈 화면이 아니라, 전투가 계속 진행되는 상태에서 플레이어가 하단 업그레이드와 보상 수령을 반복하는 idle RPG 화면을 목표로 한다.

## Character Prompt Anchor

- Golden-yellow hamster hero with cream belly and muzzle.
- Tiny round ears, pink cheek blush, simple cute face.
- Thick dark chocolate outline and flat Spine-atlas-like 2D shapes.
- Toy-like rounded proportions, no realistic fur or complex anatomy.
- Must feel like the same visual family as the existing Hamzzi/Ninster hamster assets.

## Scene Direction

- Cozy mushroom forest battlefield.
- Rounded grassy platform, oversized mushrooms, soft green/yellow sunlight.
- Small mushroom/slime enemies approach while the hamster auto-attacks.
- Combat state should be visible: hit VFX, damage numbers, drops, HP bars, cooldown rings.

## UI Direction

- Top: compact currency, stage/wave, power indicator.
- Left: quest/mission shortcuts.
- Right: vertical skill/boost buttons with cooldown rings.
- Bottom: thumb-reachable upgrade panel with four stat buttons and one larger golden grow/claim CTA.
- HUD frames combat without hiding the hamster or enemies.
